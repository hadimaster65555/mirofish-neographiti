### 6. ANTAM had just secured an important domestic gold supply agreement

The most material March corporate story I found was the Gold Sales and Purchase Agreement with Merdeka Group entities:

- announced by Merdeka on `2026-03-05`
- also covered by ANTARA on `2026-03-04`

What the sources agree on:

My read:

Even with the public-source discrepancy on volume framing, the underlying signal is clear: ANTAM entered March 2026 with a meaningful new domestic gold-supply arrangement, and that was one of the most important non-retail ANTAM developments still relevant by `2026-03-16`.
